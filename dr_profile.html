Dr. Monica Pahuja (Placeholder)

Dr. Monica Pahuja graduated in 2001 having trained at the Alfred Hospital. Over the last 12 years she has developed a career in women’s imaging. She was previously the Director of Medical Imaging at the Mercy Hospital for Women and worked at the Royal Women’s Hospital and tertiary obstetric unit at Monash Ultrasound for Women. Dr. Pahuja was a senior radiologist for Breast Imaging Victoria over the last 10 years and has expertise in breast imaging.

She currently holds the position of Head of Women’s Imaging at Monash Health and is a partner at Direct Radiology, Melbourne.

Dr. Pahuja is the Chairperson for the Board of Examiners (DDU) for the Australasian Society for Ultrasound in Medicine and is on the US reference group for the Royal College of Radiologists. Dr. Pahuja is also on the Faculty for the Royal Melbourne Hospital teaching program for radiology trainees.

She brings with her a vast experience of obstetrics and gynecological imaging and mammography. Her other areas of interest are paediatrics and body imaging.
